Mojito

Ingredients

50 mlWhite Rum
8-10Mint leaves
12 1/2 mlSugar Syrup
25 mlLime Juice
2Mint sprigs

How to make a Mojito

  1. Add the white rum to a highball glass.
  2. Add 8â€“10 mint leaves and sugar syrup and lime juice.
  3. Muddle with bar spoon.
  4. Add crushed ice and a splash of soda.
  5. Mix drink down with bar spoon. Taste.
  6. Top up with more crushed ice.
  7. Slap 2 mint sprigs to release essence and put into drink.
  8. Add small splash of soda and straw.

Cosmopolitan

Ingredients

37 1/2 mlCitrus Vodka
12 1/2 mlTriple Sec
30 mlCranberry Juice
1/2squeezed lemon
12 1/2 mlSugar Syrup

How to make a Cosmopolitan

  1. Chill coupe glass with soda water.
  2. Add all ingredients to mixing tin with ice.
  3. Shake for 10 to 15 seconds.
  4. Fine strain into the chilled coupe glass.
  5. Flame an orange peel and place in drink as garnish.

Bloody Mary

Ingredients

37 1/2 mlPeppered Vodka
15 mlSweet Vermouth
35 mlTomato Juice, splash Red Wine
6 splashesWorcestershire sauce
10 dashesTobasco sauce
25 mlLemon Juice
10 dashesSalt
10 dashesPepper
3Cucumber slices

How to make a Bloody Mary

  1. Chill a highball glass with ice.
  2. Pour all the ingredients, apart from red wine, into mixing tin and add ice.
  3. Roll the mixing tin for 15 seconds. Taste.
  4. Add ice to glass and single strain the drink into a glass.
  5. Add a splash of red wine and a dash of pepper.
  6. Garnish with 3 cucumber slices.
